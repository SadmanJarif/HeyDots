Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their sites.

2. How We Use Cookies

HeyDots uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential website functionality and security
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Improving our website performance and user experience
  • Providing personalized content and recommendations
  • Marketing and advertising purposes (with your consent)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

  • Session management and security
  • Load balancing and website functionality
  • Form submission and user authentication

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Page load times and error tracking
  • User journey and behavior analysis

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization features.

  • Language and region preferences
  • User interface customizations
  • Chat widget and support features

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaign effectiveness.

  • Targeted advertising and retargeting
  • Social media integration
  • Campaign performance tracking

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device. These include:

Google Analytics

Website traffic and user behavior analysis

Cal.com

Meeting scheduling and calendar integration

Social Media Platforms

Social sharing and integration features

Marketing Tools

Email marketing and lead tracking

5. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are essential for website functionality during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period (typically 30 days to 2 years) or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences across visits.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can block, delete, or receive notifications about cookies.

Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you can choose which types of cookies to accept through our cookie consent banner.

Opt-Out Links

For third-party cookies, you can often opt out directly through the service provider's website or privacy settings.

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can disable cookies, please note that doing so may affect your experience on our website:

  • Some website features may not function properly
  • You may need to re-enter information on each visit
  • Personalized content and recommendations may not be available
  • We may not be able to remember your preferences

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
